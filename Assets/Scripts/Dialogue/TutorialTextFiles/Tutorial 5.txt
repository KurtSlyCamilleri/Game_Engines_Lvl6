|| Ladders can be hung onto after jumping and climbed. Ahead is also a Lock Picking puzzle, get close and Left Mouse Button click on it to start the puzzle. You have a limited amount of Lock Picks which can be generated by collecting Scrap Metal. To beat this puzzle, use WASD to move the Lock Pick and Space Bar to grab and let go of the Lock's Pins, determine the correct placement of the Pins using visuals then let go to place them. Place them all correctly to unlock. Place too many incorrectly and you may break a Lock Pick. ||
